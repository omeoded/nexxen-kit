About the Team - Phobos team

The Phobos team manages two powerful tools that help advertisers better understand and reach their audiences: Discovery and the integrated DSP-DMP platform. Together, these systems make it easier to create successful ad campaigns by providing valuable insights and precise targeting.

About the Products

Discovery Platform

Discovery is a sophisticated audience analytics and insights platform designed to help advertisers uncover actionable data about their target audiences. It empowers users to analyze audience behavior, segment effectively, and refine their marketing strategies with precision. Discovery simplifies the process of improving marketing strategies, by his clear visuals and easy integration into other tools.

Demand-Side Platform (DSP)

Our Demand-Side Platform (DSP) is a powerful, data-driven platform designed to help advertisers efficiently purchase and manage digital ad inventory across multiple channels. The platform provides real-time analytics, audience segmentation, and performance optimization, enabling advertisers to target the right audiences with the right messages at scale.

By focusing on data processing, targeting, and analytics, our DSP ensures that our clients can maximize their ROI and make smarter decisions based on real-time insights. The platform empowers advertisers to run programmatic campaigns across a wide variety of channels, including display, video, and connected TV.

The Data Management Platform (DMP) is a crucial component of this ecosystem, tightly integrated with the DSP to provide unparalleled audience targeting capabilities.

About the Role: Senior Backend Engineer

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our growing DSP-DMP platform. In this role, you will help build and optimize the backend systems that power our cutting-edge advertising platform, used by some of the biggest global brands.

As a Senior Backend Engineer, you'll be responsible for designing and developing scalable backend services that enable advanced campaign setups, data processing, and analytics. You will collaborate with engineers, data scientists, and product teams to enhance the platform's capabilities in areas such as campaign setup, targeting, audience segmentation, reporting, and real-time decision-making.

Interview Preparation Guide

What to Expect

The interview will be a friendly, conversational session designed to get to know you better both personally and professionally. We aim to understand your background, problem-solving approach, and technical knowledge.

Personal & Professional Background

We'll discuss your experiences, goals, and how you've approached challenges in past roles.

Technical Knowledge

Expect general software engineering questions with an emphasis on backend development and web technologies. While no live coding will be involved, we may dive into technical concepts and design questions.

Architecture & Design

Be prepared to explain and present your thought process for designing systems. You'll be asked to create architecture diagrams (use any tool you prefer, such as a whiteboard or a digital tool) to illustrate your ideas.

Duration: 30-60 minutes
