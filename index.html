DSP and DMP Integration

Nexxen's Demand-Side Platform (DSP) empowers advertisers to run programmatic campaigns across a wide variety of channels, including display, video, and connected TV. The platform is designed for precision targeting, real-time bidding, and optimization, enabling users to achieve maximum ROI on their ad spend.

The Data Management Platform (DMP) is a crucial component of this ecosystem, tightly integrated with the DSP to provide unparalleled audience targeting capabilities.

Key Features of the DSP-DMP Integration

Unified Audience Profiles

The DMP aggregates first-party, second-party, and third-party data into unified audience profiles. This data is seamlessly shared with the DSP to enable precise targeting in real-time.

Advanced Audience Segmentation

Users can define custom audience segments in the DMP based on demographics, behaviors, purchase intent, or other attributes. These segments can be activated directly within the DSP for campaign targeting.

Cross-Channel Targeting

By leveraging audience insights from the DMP, the DSP enables advertisers to execute cross-channel campaigns that ensure consistency and relevance across devices and platforms.

Real-Time Data Activation

The integration ensures that audience data from the DMP can be applied in real-time bidding (RTB) scenarios, allowing advertisers to bid dynamically based on up-to-date user behavior and preferences.

Lookalike Modeling

The DMP leverages machine learning to identify high-performing audience segments and expand reach with lookalike modeling. This enhances targeting capabilities by finding similar users likely to engage.

Privacy and Compliance

Both the DSP and DMP are designed to comply with data privacy regulations such as GDPR and CCPA, ensuring advertisers can leverage data responsibly and transparently.

How This Adds Value

The seamless DSP-DMP integration provides advertisers with a competitive edge by allowing them to leverage robust audience analytics and actionable insights in their campaigns. The ability to use real-time data ensures relevance, while cross-channel targeting boosts engagement and performance.
